Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 February 2026

subTerra is a metal detecting log and find tracking application.

Information collected by subTerra

You can use subTerra to track the following types of information:

  • Location data to track the position of your finds.
  • Location data to track your movement as you detect.
  • Location data to record the position of your photographs.

Your information

  • None of your information is ever sent to the developer.
  • None of your personal data is ever shared or sold to a third party.

How subTerra uses your information

subTerra uses collected data solely to:

  • Store and manage user detecting history.
  • Convert precise, anonymous GPS coordinates to what3words three word addresses.

Third-party services

The following services are used by subTerra:

  • what3words api: Converts GPS coordinates into three word addresses.
  • Surge: Used for mathematical calculations.
  • NominatimKit: Provides the map search functionality using OpenStreetMap data.

Data storage and security

  • All of your data is stored locally on your device.
  • No automatic transmission of any user identifiable data ever occurs.

User rights and controls

  • You can modify location permissions at any time in Settings.
  • All digs, finds, images and landowner details can be deleted at any time.
